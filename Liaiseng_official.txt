Lai-Hsiang International Co., Ltd., or Lai-Hsiang International for short, is a Taiwanese subsidiary of TIS Co., Ltd., a Japanese company, which was approved and registered by the Ministry of Economic Affairs on May 18, 2012. Its main business includes advertising agency services for Japanese animation and games, film broadcasting licensing, copyright trading, and the sale and purchase of DVD-related products. It also assists its parent company and related companies in providing film and video post-production editing and film quality inspection.
